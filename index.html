Unit 1: Getting Started

Introduction & Mindset, DAACS

Introduction & Mindset Discussion

In this discussion, I explored the concept of growth mindset versus fixed mindset and how our beliefs about our abilities can significantly impact our success. I learned that embracing challenges, persisting through obstacles, and viewing effort as a path to mastery are crucial aspects of a growth mindset.

DAACS Assignment

The Diagnostic Assessment and Achievement of College Skills (DAACS) helped me identify my academic strengths and areas for improvement. The assessment evaluated my self-regulated learning skills, including time management, motivation, and study strategies. This self-awareness provided a foundation for my growth throughout the course.

Key Takeaway

Understanding that intelligence and abilities are not fixed traits but can be developed through dedication and hard work transformed my approach to learning. I now view challenges as opportunities for growth rather than threats to my self-image.

2

Unit 2: Setting Direction

Goals & Motivations

Goals & Motivations Assignment

This assignment helped me articulate both my short-term academic goals and long-term career aspirations. I learned the importance of setting SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) and connecting them to my intrinsic motivations.

I identified that my primary motivation for pursuing education is to enhance my professional skills and create more opportunities for career advancement. Understanding my "why" has helped me stay focused during challenging times.

Key Takeaway

Setting clear, well-defined goals with specific timeframes and action steps keeps me accountable and motivated. When my goals are aligned with my deeper motivations and values, I'm more resilient in the face of obstacles and setbacks.

Unit 3: Time Management

Big Rocks & Scheduling

What are Your Big Rocks? Discussion

This discussion introduced the concept of "big rocks" â€“ the most important priorities in my life that should be scheduled first. I identified my education, family relationships, health, and career development as my big rocks.

I learned that effective time management isn't about finding more time but making deliberate choices about how to use the time I have.

Organizing Your Schedule Assignment

In this assignment, I created a comprehensive weekly schedule that incorporated my big rocks while also accounting for regular responsibilities and personal needs. I learned techniques for prioritizing tasks, avoiding procrastination, and maintaining work-life balance.

Key Takeaway

Scheduling my priorities rather than prioritizing what's already on my schedule has been a game-changer. By blocking time for my most important goals first and protecting that time, I've become more productive and focused while reducing stress and last-minute rushes.

Unit 5: Career Exploration

Industry Trends & Interview Prep

Preparing for Your Informational Interview Discussion

This discussion helped me prepare for conducting an informational interview with a professional in my field of interest. I learned how to identify potential interviewees, craft thoughtful questions, and make a positive impression.

Investigating Trends in Your Career Field Assignment

For this assignment, I researched current trends, challenges, and opportunities in my chosen career field. This investigation gave me valuable insights into how the industry is evolving and what skills I need to develop to remain competitive.

Key Takeaway

Proactively exploring career trends and connecting with professionals in my field provides real-world context for my education. This knowledge helps me make strategic decisions about skill development and specialization that align with industry demands and future opportunities.

Unit 6: Professional Connections

Networking & Informational Interview

Networking & Teamwork Discussion

In this discussion, I explored effective networking strategies and the importance of teamwork in professional settings. I learned that building authentic relationships based on mutual value is more effective than transactional networking.

Informational Interview Assignment

I conducted an informational interview with a professional in my field and gained valuable insights about career paths, day-to-day responsibilities, industry challenges, and advice for newcomers. This experience helped me validate my career choice and refine my professional goals.

Key Takeaway

Networking is not just about collecting contacts but building genuine relationships where both parties benefit. The informational interview gave me insider knowledge that I couldn't have gained from research alone, demonstrating the power of learning directly from experienced professionals.

Unit 7: University Resources

Support Services

Support Services Summary Assignment

For this assignment, I researched and summarized the various support services available to UMGC students. I discovered resources for academic support, career guidance, financial assistance, technical help, and personal well-being that I wasn't fully aware of before.

I developed a plan for utilizing specific resources that align with my needs and goals, particularly the writing center, library services, and career services.

Key Takeaway

Taking full advantage of university resources can significantly enhance my academic success and overall college experience. These services represent an investment the university has made in my success, and utilizing them strategically gives me a substantial return on my tuition investment.

Personal Growth
