Unit 1: Getting Started

Introduction & Mindset, DAACS

What Did I Learn?

In Unit 1, I was introduced to the concept of self-regulated learning through the DAACS assessment. As a Navy veteran transitioning to civilian life and a new career as a data analyst, this assessment provided valuable insights into my learning strengths and areas for improvement. I discovered that while I excel at creating a distraction-free study environment, I face challenges with time management, help-seeking, and test anxiety.

My DAACS report highlighted how my military background influenced my approach to learning. After 23 years in the Navy as an Aviation Machinists Mate, I developed a habit of operating under pressure and prioritizing others' needs over my own. This assessment helped me understand that what worked in a high-tempo military environment might not be the most effective approach for academic success.

The mindset discussion revealed that I possess a strong growth mindset in many areas, particularly in my professional capabilities, but also showed how my background as a non-native English speaker sometimes affects my confidence in academic settings.

How Will I Use This Moving Forward?

Moving forward, I plan to leverage the DAACS assessment results as a roadmap for improving my academic strategies. Now that I understand my strengths and weaknesses, I can take specific actions to address areas needing development:

  • For help-seeking, I'll practice overcoming my reluctance to ask questions by reminding myself that seeking assistance isn't a burden but can actually benefit both myself and others. I'll use the resources UMGC provides, such as tutoring services and the Effective Writing Center.
  • For time management, I'll implement structured planning using the techniques suggested in the DAACS resources, including creating consistent study schedules and prioritizing assignments based on importance and deadline.
  • For test anxiety, I'll utilize relaxation techniques before and during high-stress situations and focus on consistent preparation rather than last-minute cramming.
Key Takeaways
  • Self-awareness is the foundation of personal growth. The DAACS assessment provided objective feedback that helped me see patterns in my learning behaviors that I might not have recognized on my own.
  • My background in the Navy gave me valuable skills like problem-solving and persistence, but also created habits that may need adjustment in an academic environment.
  • As I mentioned in my introduction post, my approach to troubleshooting aircraft engines—breaking down complex problems into manageable parts—can be applied to my academic journey. Just as I would "take a step back, assess the situation, and figure out how everything was supposed to come together" when faced with mechanical issues, I can apply this same methodical approach to overcoming learning challenges.
  • The path from military service to data science represents not just a career change but an opportunity to combine my technical expertise with new academic knowledge in ways that align with my natural curiosity and problem-solving abilities.
  • I recognize that this self-assessment is not just about identifying weaknesses but about building on my existing strengths to create a solid foundation for success at UMGC and in my data science career.

Unit 2: Setting Direction

Goals & Motivations

Goals & Motivations Assignment

This assignment helped me articulate both my short-term academic goals and long-term career aspirations. I learned the importance of setting SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) and connecting them to my intrinsic motivations.

I identified that my primary motivation for pursuing education is to enhance my professional skills and create more opportunities for career advancement. Understanding my "why" has helped me stay focused during challenging times.

Key Takeaway

Setting clear, well-defined goals with specific timeframes and action steps keeps me accountable and motivated. When my goals are aligned with my deeper motivations and values, I'm more resilient in the face of obstacles and setbacks.

Unit 3: Time Management

Big Rocks & Scheduling

What are Your Big Rocks? Discussion

This discussion introduced the concept of "big rocks" – the most important priorities in my life that should be scheduled first. I identified my education, family relationships, health, and career development as my big rocks.

I learned that effective time management isn't about finding more time but making deliberate choices about how to use the time I have.

Organizing Your Schedule Assignment

In this assignment, I created a comprehensive weekly schedule that incorporated my big rocks while also accounting for regular responsibilities and personal needs. I learned techniques for prioritizing tasks, avoiding procrastination, and maintaining work-life balance.

Key Takeaway

Scheduling my priorities rather than prioritizing what's already on my schedule has been a game-changer. By blocking time for my most important goals first and protecting that time, I've become more productive and focused while reducing stress and last-minute rushes.

Unit 4: Building Support

Success Network

Engage with your Success Network Assignment

This assignment prompted me to identify and map out my personal success network – the people and resources who can support me throughout my academic journey. I learned that having a strong support system is crucial for overcoming challenges and achieving goals.

I identified mentors, peers, family members, and institutional resources that form my success network, and I developed strategies for strengthening these connections.

Key Takeaway

No one succeeds entirely on their own. Building and maintaining relationships with people who can provide different types of support – emotional encouragement, academic guidance, professional connections, and practical assistance – creates a safety net that increases my resilience and chances of success.

Unit 5: Career Exploration

Industry Trends & Interview Prep

Preparing for Your Informational Interview Discussion

This discussion helped me prepare for conducting an informational interview with a professional in my field of interest. I learned how to identify potential interviewees, craft thoughtful questions, and make a positive impression.

Investigating Trends in Your Career Field Assignment

For this assignment, I researched current trends, challenges, and opportunities in my chosen career field. This investigation gave me valuable insights into how the industry is evolving and what skills I need to develop to remain competitive.

Key Takeaway

Proactively exploring career trends and connecting with professionals in my field provides real-world context for my education. This knowledge helps me make strategic decisions about skill development and specialization that align with industry demands and future opportunities.

Unit 6: Professional Connections

Networking & Informational Interview

Networking & Teamwork Discussion

In this discussion, I explored effective networking strategies and the importance of teamwork in professional settings. I learned that building authentic relationships based on mutual value is more effective than transactional networking.

Informational Interview Assignment

I conducted an informational interview with a professional in my field and gained valuable insights about career paths, day-to-day responsibilities, industry challenges, and advice for newcomers. This experience helped me validate my career choice and refine my professional goals.

Key Takeaway

Networking is not just about collecting contacts but building genuine relationships where both parties benefit. The informational interview gave me insider knowledge that I couldn't have gained from research alone, demonstrating the power of learning directly from experienced professionals.

Unit 7: University Resources

Support Services

Support Services Summary Assignment

For this assignment, I researched and summarized the various support services available to UMGC students. I discovered resources for academic support, career guidance, financial assistance, technical help, and personal well-being that I wasn't fully aware of before.

I developed a plan for utilizing specific resources that align with my needs and goals, particularly the writing center, library services, and career services.

Key Takeaway

Taking full advantage of university resources can significantly enhance my academic success and overall college experience. These services represent an investment the university has made in my success, and utilizing them strategically gives me a substantial return on my tuition investment.

Looking Forward: Applying What I've Learned

This course has equipped me with essential skills and knowledge that extend beyond academic success to professional development and personal growth. As I move forward in my educational journey at UMGC, I'll continue to build upon these foundations.

Academic Application

I will implement the time management strategies and study techniques I've learned to maximize my academic performance in future courses. By maintaining a growth mindset, utilizing university resources, and consistently applying effective learning strategies, I aim to achieve and maintain academic excellence.

Professional Development

I will continue expanding my professional network and staying informed about industry trends. The career exploration skills and networking strategies I've developed will help me identify opportunities, make informed career decisions, and position myself competitively in the job market.

Personal Growth

I will maintain balance by prioritizing my "big rocks" and nurturing my support network. The self-awareness and reflection skills I've developed in this course will serve me well as I navigate challenges, set new goals, and continue growing as a lifelong learner.
